Who am I?

I am a PhD student at the University of Iceland under the supervision of Sigurður Örn Stefánsson. My current area of research is on random walks, planar graphs and the scaling limit of random trees. Perviously I completed the joint program “M2 de Mathématiques Fondamentales” by Sorbonne Université, Université de Paris, and Université Sorbonne Paris Nord, where I focused mainly on subjects relating to number theory. After finishing my courses, I did a five month research internship at the university of Luxembourg under the supervision of professor Gabor Wiese on the asymptotic behavior of the coefficient field of modular forms modulo $p$.
